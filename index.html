App Overview

What is Formula-1 app?

Formula-1 is a web app designed for iOS and Android.You can use this as a informative application about Formula-1. Basic F-1 news such as standing of drivers, winnings of races and all information about races.Actual news of teams and drivers and many more.

Features

  • Easy Browsing

    Formula 1 app operates smoothly and efficiently. Scrolling through all of the different standings of drivers is quick and easy.

  • Latest News

    From the latest news and exclusive interviews about the hottest rumors to results of training, qualifying and race - with this app you are well informed around the clock.

  • Drivers

    Team and Driver Standings.All information about drivers and teams such as winnings races and qualification.Wan to know more just download.

  • Races

    Basic info about race. User can see circuit lenght, race distance, lap record

Screens

Formula-1 app for your iPod touch, iPad, and iPhone gives users the chance to enjoy some photos and share their own in the process. This app is simple, smooth, and very entertaining.
