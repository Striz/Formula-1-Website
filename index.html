App Overview


Formula-1 is a web app designed for iOS and Android.You can use this as a informative application about Formula-1. Basic Formula-1 news. You can check standing of drivers, winnings of races and all information about races. Actual news of teams and drivers and much more.

Features

  • Easy Browsing

    Formula 1 app operates smoothly and efficiently. Scrolling through all the different standings of drivers,teams is quick and easy.

  • Latest News

    Get the latest news and exclusive interviews. The hottest rumors to results of training. qualifying and race - with this app you are well informed around the clock.

  • Drivers

    Team and Driver Standings. All information about drivers and teams such as winnings races and qualification. Want to know more just download.

  • Races

    Basic info about race.
    User can see circuit length, race distance, lap record.

Screens

Formula-1 app is for your iPod touch, iPad, and iPhone. For users gives the chance to enjoy some photos and images from the races. This app is simple, smooth, and very entertaining.
